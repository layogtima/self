OUR BATTLE CRY

THE MANIFESTO

  1. Design should evoke emotion, not serve function.
  2. Embrace color as rebellion against minimalism.
  3. Patterns are political statements against the bland.
  4. Technology is not just for solving problems; it's for challenging perception.
  5. Rationality is boring; absurdity is liberation.

"The Memphis Movement frees design from the tyranny of good taste. We aren't trying to solve problems – we are trying to create them."
