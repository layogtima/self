THE GUIDE TO TRADING

"The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy has this to say about interstellar trading: It's mostly about knowing where to be, when to be there, and what not to be carrying when the Vogons arrive."

  • Buy low, sell high - Different planets have different prices for goods
  • Travel smartly - Each journey costs money and time
  • Embrace improbability - Random events will occur
  • Build reputation - Different species will offer different deals
  • Always know where your towel is - Essential for any galactic hitchhiker

Your goal is to accumulate 1 million Altairian Dollars and locate Magrathea to commission a new Earth.
