Level 5

The Sky Observatory

A light-drenched solarium for contemplation, crowned with a BIPV glass skylight, offering a top-down view of the five-story green core.

Level 4

The Hanging Gardens

Residential pods and a community library, partitioned by floor-to-ceiling hydroponic walls. Bridges cross through the central tree's upper canopy.

Level 3

The Living Canopy

Flexible living pods in a lightweight bamboo superstructure, each with a private balcony garden, creating a cascade of greenery.

Level 2

The Community Hearth

The social heart. A communal kitchen and lounge space that opens into the main garden level of the central atrium, featuring an aquaponics pond.

Level 1

The Root Cellar

The functional bedrock. A fully earth-bermed utility core, workshop, and mycelium lab, built from rammed-earth tires.
