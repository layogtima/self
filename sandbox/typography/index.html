GUIDELINES

Size Matters

Headings should be 2-3× larger than body text to establish proper visual hierarchy.

Heading

Body text size establishes the baseline for all other text.

Line Length

Maintain 45-75 characters per line for optimal readability in paragraphs.

This line length is comfortable for the human eye to scan and helps readers maintain focus as they move down the text.

Font Contrast

Pair fonts that complement, not compete. One distinctive, one neutral.

Display Font

Clean, neutral supporting font that doesn't distract from the display font.

Weight Distribution

Vary font weights to create hierarchy and points of emphasis.

Bold (700) for emphasis

Regular (400) for body text

Light (300) for supporting text

Consistent Scale

Use a modular scale for font sizing to maintain visual harmony.

Large (24px)

Medium (18px)

Base (16px)

Small (14px)

Purpose Alignment

Match font characteristics with your content's purpose and tone.

Serif: Traditional, authoritative

Sans-serif: Modern, clean

Monospace: Technical, precise

Script: Elegant, personal
